Welcome to California!

A place of boundless possibilities, majestic redwoods, and sunshine! Warmly lit by the Pacific Coast, California presents a singular fusion of breathtaking scenery, energetic cities, and a diverse range of cultural experiences.

The population of California is a diverse cross-section of the world's cultures. The food, celebrations, and customs of the state all reflect this diversity. California has an extremely diverse population, which is a result of a combination of Native American, European, Asian, and Latino influences as well as immigration.

Thriving Cities and Cultural Gems:

  1. San Francisco: A city by the bay known for its iconic Golden Gate Bridge, cable cars, and diverse neighborhoods like Chinatown and Haight-Ashbury. Sample fresh Dungeness crab at Fisherman's Wharf, savor dim sum in Chinatown, or indulge in Michelin-starred restaurants.
  2. Hollywood is located in Los Angeles, which is also the global hub for pop culture, music, and film. The weather in Los Angeles is sunny and ideal for outdoor activities. Climb Runyon Canyon for sweeping views of the city.
  3. Sacramento is the energetic capital city of California, boasting a thriving arts scene and a rich history. To get a peek of state government, visit the California State Capitol or Discover Sutter's Fort, a recreated fort from the gold rush era.

The population of California is a diverse cross-section of the world's cultures. The food, celebrations, and customs of the state all reflect this diversity. California's multicultural spirit is a defining feature that you will see when visiting its major cities, influenced by everything from Native American influences to European settlers to the influx of Asian and Latino cultures!
