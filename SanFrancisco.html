Welcome to San Francisco!

San Francisco was incorporated on April 15, 1850. Currently, San Francisco has a population of 865,933. You can find San Francisco in the Bay Area of California. In San Francisco you will find a dense urban city. The average household income level in San Francisco is $178,742, well above California's average of $119,149 for household income.

About San Francisco

San Francisco is a city where colorful neighborhoods, refreshing fog, and famous landmarks like the Golden Gate Bridge come together. San Francisco, also known as "The City by the Bay," is chock full of natural beauty, history, and cultural attractions. Take a cable car ride up a steep hill, explore the remnants of the Gold Rush era, or visit renowned museums like the de Young. Beyond its busy cityscape, explore the natural beauty of Golden Gate Park, the cool embrace of the Pacific Ocean, and hidden gardens.

Fun Locations

  1. Alcatraz Island: Learn about the history of the island, its inmates, and the harsh living conditions endured by prisoners.
  2. Golden Gate Bridge: The most iconic landmark of San Francisco, walk or bike across the bridge for breathtaking panoramic views of the city and the bay
  3. Twin Peaks: Hike or drive to the summit of Twin Peaks for unparalleled panoramic views of the entire city.
  4. Fisherman's Wharf: A lively waterfront that offers a variety of attractions such as fresh seafood restaurants and souvenir shops and Pier 39.
  5. Ghirardelli Square: A historic chocolate factory turned into a vibrant square with shops and restaurants.
